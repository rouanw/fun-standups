Yesterday, today, blockers

Each person lists what they did yesterday, what they'll do today and what is in their way

This is a classic standup format. Each person give an update on what they did yesterday (i.e. since the last standup), what they’ll be doing today and whether there are any blockers making it difficult for their work to progress.

  • This is a tried-and-tested format and a good one to fall back on
