Last three commits

Read your last three commit messages to the rest of the team.

If you are a team member who doesn't make commits, read the last three email titles or the names of the last three files that you saved. If those messages are not self-explanatory, talk briefly about the context.

  • You'll get an insight into what others are working on right now. It can be especially interesting to see the messages from team members who do a different job from yourself. For example, if you're a programmer, the day-to-day work of a product manager might be unfamiliar to you.

Contributed by RabeaGleissner.
