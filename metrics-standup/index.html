Show me the numbers!

Take a look at your team's key product metrics (graphs, totals etc.)

Instead of looking at user stories or an Agile wall, have your standup while someone shares a dashboard showing the numbers your team cares about most. This dashboard could contain a graph showing sales pipeline conversions, a table showing the top errors in the last hour, or the number of new user registrations in the day.

  • Helps the team connect with the bigger goals they're trying to achieve.
  • Could reveal if the metrics being used to measure success are flawed, or maybe not visible enough to the team.
